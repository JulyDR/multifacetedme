About

Always Learning. Always Exploring

Hi there!

It's Julia here

I am a curious soul in my mid-thirties who's currently on a year-long adventure of self-discovery. For the past six years, the Dominican Republic has been my vibrant home, where I've soaked up the sun, learned the language (somewhat!), and explored every hidden gem I could find.

Now, it's time for a new chapter!

I'm taking a break from the traditional work routine to figure out where life wants to take me next. This year is all about introspection, exploration (both geographical and personal), learning new skills, and prioritizing my well-being.

The beauty of this year is the lack of a plan. This year is all about embracing the unknown (something that actually scares me a lot!). Join me as I learn, explore, and figure out what life has in store next!

My Passions!

Wellness:
 Finding inner peace and maintaining physical health are key. You'll find me on my yoga mat, trying to master meditation (wish me luck!), or catching some waves while kitesurfing.

Learning:
 I'm a lifelong learner, always hungry for new knowledge. This year, I'm diving headfirst into the world of web development and web design, figuring out if i can turn it into a future career. But I am also keen on learning about nutrition, finances or try out new sports (perhaps diving?).

Exploration:
 I crave new experiences and fresh perspectives. Whether it's exploring European cities, taking surf lessons or chasing waterfalls, I'm up for anything!

Follow Along!

Dreaming of escaping the ordinary? If you're curious about the expat life, a digital nomad wannabe, or just someone who enjoys a good adventure, Multifaceted Me can be your companion and inspiration station!. I'll share not just my exciting journey, but also practical tips and hacks to help you turn your dreams into reality. Join me as I explore new possibilities, learn new skills, and find my perfect place in this big, beautiful world.

Feel free to leave comments, ask questions, and share your own experiences by connecting with me through social media or email.
